Cuzco, The Fifth Wonder Of The World.



WHY CUZCO?

  • Machu Picchu is an ancient Inca citadel that is known for its remarkable architecture.
  • Cuzco was the historical capital of the Inca Empire
  • Cuzco is nestled in the Andes Mountains which provides breathtaking panoramic views of the surrounding mountain ranges.


FUN FACTS

  1. Machu Picchu was designated a UNESCO World Heritage Site in 1983.
  2. Machu Picchu is famous for its Intihuatana Stone, which is a granite ritual stone that is believed to have been used as an astronomical clock or calendar by the Incas.
  3. One of the most popular ways to reach Machu Picchu is by trekking the Inca Trail, which is a multi-day hiking trail that follows an ancient Inca road.


WHEN?

  • Weather: The dry season, which is generally considered the best time to visit, runs from May to September.
  • If you prefer to avoid large crowds, you may want to consider visiting Machu Picchu during the shoulder seasons of April and October.
  • Festivals and events: Cuzco is known for its vibrant cultural festivals and events, such as Inti Raymi, the Festival of the Sun, which takes place in Cusco in June.
